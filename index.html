Meet Luca, Your Supply Chain Assistant

Luca is a supportive AI designed to help you explore the basics of supply chain management within The Fresh Connection simulation. While Luca isn’t an expert model, he uses foundational insights to guide you in areas like inventory management, logistics planning, and demand alignment.

With Luca, you’ll receive simple, practical advice that can help improve key supply chain metrics within the game. His focus is on helping you learn and implement straightforward strategies to support the goals of your supply chain role.

Click on the chatbot icon to start a conversation with Luca and get basic tips and suggestions for navigating supply chain challenges in The Fresh Connection!

Frequently Asked Questions

How does Luca help with demand alignment?
Luca’s context includes general strategies for adjusting inventory levels based on forecasted demand. He provides basic tips to help you balance stock levels and minimize shortages.

What insights does Luca offer on logistics planning?
While not advanced, Luca can guide you in choosing efficient transportation methods and keeping logistics costs manageable within game objectives.

How does Luca handle disruptions in supply?
Luca can suggest a few basic strategies for creating resilience, like adjusting safety stock and selecting more reliable suppliers, though his guidance is broad and context-based.

Does Luca coordinate with other roles?
While Luca’s insights focus on supply chain basics, he considers interactions with purchasing and production to help you keep the flow of goods smooth within The Fresh Connection framework.
